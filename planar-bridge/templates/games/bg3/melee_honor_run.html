Melee only rules

  • No consumables (potions, scrolls, grenades) except when part of the class.
    • Exception: Dyes, Camp Supplies and Tools.
    • Exception: Wizards can learn from scrolls and drunk monks can drink alcohol.
  • Can't change gear in the middle of a fight.
  • Can't equip more than 3 items of rarity 'rare' or above.
  • Must use a melee character:
    • Can use any spell that deals 0 damage.
    • Can only use spells and cantrips that deal damage if range is below 10 m.
    • Can use any spell provided by an item.
    • Can't equip a ranged weapon.
    • Can't use Produce Flame, Lightning Bolt or Sunbeam.
      • Small exceptions can be made for class features and always prepared spells.
    • Can't use illithid powers.
